Daysie Restaurant





Description

Catch up with friends and family or take yourself on a date to this casual and breezy all-day bar on MG Road, owned by partners Anirudh Kheny, Neeti Gokhalay Kheny and Atul Sunder. Daysie promises a creative and global menu of food with influences from around the world. Whether it’s a fusion of multiple European cuisines or a new take on an Indian classic, there’s something for every kind of taste and palette. Perhaps you could start your meal with a hearty soup—such as the lobster bisque or the mutton paaya—and then move onto the delightful munchies. The Bohri kheema samosas, the masala and kheema khakhra, and the salmon papdi chaat are all good options. For the mains, choose from a wide and flavourful array of burgers and pizzas, burritos and kebabs, curries and rice. Daysie’s program of carefully crafted cocktails reflects the fun and quirky vibe of the restaurant, from G&T to special dessert cocktails. The restaurant has both indoor and outdoor seating that stars from a stone-paved pathway and goes up to the colourful mezzanine level inside.
